About Gem

  • A competition was held in 2002 to design a new museum to house ancient Egyptian artifacts.
  • The winning design was chosen in 2003 and construction began in 2005.
  • The project faced delays due to environmental, financial, and political reasons.
  • Construction resumed in 2014 and is nearing completion.
  • Upon opening (estimated mid-2024), GEM will be the world’s largest archaeological museum.
  • It will house over 100,000 artifacts, including King Tut’s entire collection.
  • The website you encountered, GrandEgyptianMuseum.org, is an unofficial fan site providing updates on the project.
